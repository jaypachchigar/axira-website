What Axira does

Three layers. One finance function.

Axira brings the strategic, operational, and specialist layers of finance together, so founders are not left translating between accountants, bookkeepers, advisors, and board expectations.

Strategic Core

Founder sounding board, commercial challenge and strategic finance.

The senior finance layer embedded inside the business.

  • Financial modelling
  • Board and investor materials
  • Commercial decision support
  • Growth and capital strategy
  • Embedded founder partnership
Operational Backbone

Management accounts, bookkeeping, payroll and compliance.

The infrastructure layer delivered accurately and consistently.

  • Management accounts
  • Bookkeeping and reconciliations
  • VAT and payroll
  • Year-end coordination
  • Financial reporting systems
Specialist Support

Focused project support for specific financial priorities.

Targeted expertise alongside the ongoing finance function.

  • SEIS and EIS support
  • Grant applications
  • Data rooms and due diligence
  • Diagnostics and reviews
  • Forecasting projects
Why it is different
The relationship is the product.

Most fractional finance still leaves the founder carrying the difficult decisions alone. Axira is designed to operate differently: embedded closer to the business, involved earlier in the decision-making process, and accountable to the same outcomes as the founder.

Working with us

Three ways to engage.

Most clients sit inside the Embedded Partnership. The other two structures exist for businesses that already have parts of the finance function in place.

Embedded Partnership

The full internal finance function.

Strategic and operational finance combined inside a single ongoing partnership.

  • Strategic Finance Director support
  • Monthly reporting and review
  • Bookkeeping, VAT and payroll
  • Year-end accounts and corporation tax
  • Ongoing embedded support and direct access
Strategic Advisory

The senior finance layer.

For businesses with operational finance already handled internally or externally.

  • Founder partnership and challenge
  • Strategic finance input
  • Financial modelling and unit economics review
  • Board and investor materials
  • Quarterly board attendance
Operational Foundations

The core finance infrastructure.

Accurate, consistent finance operations without the advisory layer.

  • Xero bookkeeping
  • VAT submissions
  • Monthly payroll
  • Year-end accounts and corporation tax
  • Director self-assessment
